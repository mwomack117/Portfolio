About Me

Hello! I am Michael Womack. I'm certified in Full Stack Web Development and some of my skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript, MySQL, Express, Node.js, React.js, and MongoDb. I enjoy building entire apps from scratch or optimizing existing apps. I'm a problem solver who enjoys learning new technologies. My experience has been in creating web and command line apps that communicate with databases and relay information. My goal is to combine tech and development with the ability to create unique content for clients.

In my free time, when I'm not building web applications, I enjoy outdoor rock climbing, landscape photography, and traveling. My most recent trip took me through jungles and cites across Indonesia and Thailand for six weeks.
